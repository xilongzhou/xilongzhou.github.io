<h4 class="mt-0">Research Interests</h4>
<p class="text-justify">
My primary research interests are in computer graphics with an
emphasis on computational photography and rendering. Specifically,
in recent years, I have focused on developing machine learning
techniques for image synthesis in these two fields. Overall, my goal
is to develop practical systems in a variety of computational
photography and rendering applications with the goal of representing
the world around us accurately.
</p>
